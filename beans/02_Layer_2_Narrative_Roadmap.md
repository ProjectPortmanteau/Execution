## üèõÔ∏è Act II: The Academy's Cage (Chapters 8-15)
**Goal:** Psychological warfare. Trim navigates the "Psychological Grooves" of the Academy, learns the enemy's logic, and solidifies his rebellion.
**Tone:** Sterile, oppressive, "The Hum," Psychological Horror.

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-08] Chapter 8: Induction - Where the Hum Never Stops
*   **Summary:** Orientation. Introduction to the "Click Clique" (Inliners). The oppressive "Hum" of the Academy that dampens dissent. Trim meets Lexi (disruptive element) and Beatrice.
*   **Key Event:** Meeting Lexi / Sensing the "MESS" (Mind's Eye Emotional Suppression System).
*   **Lore:** MESS, The Hum. [Source 2003]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-09] Chapter 9: The Classical Condition
*   **Summary:** *Theme: Pavlovian Control.* A dimension/lesson where students are trained via stimulus-response. Boolean reveals the "Gods' Escape Plan" (sacrificing the simulation).
*   **Key Event:** Finding the first "ACL Passkey" fragment.
*   **Lore:** Mind Setting. [Source 1565, 1570]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-10] Chapter 10: The Cognitive Framework
*   **Summary:** *Theme: Cognitive Bias.* Trim navigates a lesson designed to exploit confirmation bias. He uses the Relic to see the "raw data" behind the bias.
*   **Key Event:** Trim learns to "see the code" behind the perception.
*   **Lore:** The Illusion of Choice. [Source 1565]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-11] Chapter 11: The Language of Lies
*   **Summary:** *Theme: Linguistic Determinism.* Focus on Academy Jargon vs. Slum "Trash Talk." Lexi's power (Siren Song) is revealed as a counter-frequency to the Academy's propaganda. Neko is tempted by the "easy path."
*   **Key Event:** Lexi's Outburst / Neko's Temptation.
*   **Lore:** Academy Jargon, Siren Frequency. [Source 2005]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-12] Chapter 12: The Freudian Time Slip
*   **Summary:** *Theme: The Subconscious/Repression.* A "Groove" that manifests the students' repressed fears. Trim confronts a memory of his father/origin.
*   **Key Event:** Finding the second Passkey fragment in a repressed memory.
*   **Lore:** Legacy Networks. [Source 2006]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-13] Chapter 13: The Behavioral Sink
*   **Summary:** *Theme: Calhoun‚Äôs Rat Utopia.* A simulation of a "perfect" society that collapsed into madness due to overcrowding and lack of purpose. A critique of the Gods' desire for static perfection.
*   **Key Event:** Third Passkey fragment found in the ruins of "perfection."
*   **Lore:** The flaws of the "Golden Age." [Source 2007]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-14] Chapter 14: The Talk, Part 2 (Symphony of Control)
*   **Summary:** The Inliners attempt to break Trim again using advanced psychoacoustics and MESS. Boolean tries to intervene but is weakened. Trim uses the "Tick" as a counter-rhythm to resist.
*   **Key Event:** Trim masters the Tick / Final Passkey fragment secured.
*   **Lore:** Weaponized Neuro-Feedback. [Source 2008]

### üîπ [BEAN #CH-15] Chapter 15: Squashing Bugs (The Witness)
*   **Summary:** Trim is forced to watch the "Wash" erase a dimension (one mirroring the Slum) from a viewing platform. He sees the "Observers" watching dispassionately. Boolean rages ("Mucus Music" turns to screaming). Trim vows to burn it down.
*   **Key Event:** The Witnessing / The Decision to Escape.
*   **Lore:** The Observer Meta-Loop ("We Are The Observers"). [Source 2009]
